## Best Practices

### Subject Lines That Work
âœ… **Specific Numbers:** "1000x Faster" beats "Much Faster"
âœ… **Value Proposition:** "Save $400K/Deal" beats "Better Due Diligence"  
âœ… **Personalization:** Include company name or specific problem
âœ… **Urgency (Subtle):** "Limited Beta Spots" not "ACT NOW!!!"

### Email Body
âœ… **Short Paragraphs:** 2-3 lines max
âœ… **Bullet Points:** Easy to scan
âœ… **One CTA:** Don't overwhelm with options
âœ… **Social Proof:** Specific numbers > generic claims
âœ… **Personalization:** Show you researched them

### Follow-Up Cadence
- **Day 0:** Initial outreach
- **Day 3:** Follow-up if no response
- **Day 7:** Last attempt with value add
- **Day 14:** "Should I close your file?" email
- **Then:** Monthly newsletter to stay top-of-mind

### A/B Testing
Test these variables:
- Subject line length (short vs long)
- CTA type (demo vs trial vs call)
- Email length (brief vs detailed)
- Time sent (morning vs afternoon)
- Social proof amount

### Tracking Metrics
- Open rate: Target >25%
- Click rate: Target >10%
- Response rate: Target >5%
- Meeting booked: Target >2%
